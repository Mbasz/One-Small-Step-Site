Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 3, 2026

One Small Step ("we", "our", or "the app") is a productivity app that helps you break down goals into actionable steps and stay focused with a built-in timer. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use One Small Step, you enter task descriptions ("brain dumps") and may edit the steps the app generates. This content is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers for storage.

Focus Session Data

The app tracks timing data for your focus sessions — such as time spent on each step, total focused time, and completion status. This data is stored locally on your device only.

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to One Small Step Pro, your purchase is processed entirely by Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not collect or store your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat (see Third-Party Services below).

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses, names, or account credentials (no account creation required)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, calendar, or other device data
  • Device identifiers for advertising or tracking
  • Analytics or usage data beyond what is described above

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device capabilities.

Third-Party Services

OpenAI (via Supabase Edge Function)

When you use the AI breakdown feature, your task description is sent through a secure server-side proxy (hosted on Supabase) to OpenAI's API to generate actionable steps. Only the text you typed is sent — no personal identifiers, device information, or location data is included.

Per OpenAI's API data usage policy, data submitted through the API is not used to train their models. OpenAI may retain API inputs for up to 30 days for abuse and misuse monitoring, after which it is deleted.

We do not store your task descriptions on our servers. The Supabase Edge Function acts as a pass-through relay.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives:

  • An anonymous, randomly generated user identifier (not linked to your Apple ID or personal information)
  • Purchase receipts provided by Apple
  • Basic device information (device type, OS version) for subscription validation

RevenueCat does not receive your task content, focus session data, or any personally identifiable information. For more details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple

Standard App Store and In-App Purchase interactions are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

All your task and session data is stored locally on your device in the app's sandboxed storage. This data is protected by iOS file encryption (encrypted when your device is locked) and is included in your standard iOS backups (iCloud or local).

We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Data Retention

  • Local data: Persists on your device until you start a new session, clear it within the app, or delete the app. Uninstalling One Small Step permanently removes all local data.
  • OpenAI: Task descriptions sent for AI processing are retained by OpenAI for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, then deleted.
  • RevenueCat: Anonymous subscription data is retained as required by Apple for purchase history management.

Your Choices

  • Delete your data: Uninstall the app to permanently remove all locally stored data.
  • Avoid AI processing: You are not required to use the AI breakdown feature. If you do not generate a breakdown, no data is sent to external services.
  • Manage subscriptions: You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings (Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions).

Children's Privacy

One Small Step is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
